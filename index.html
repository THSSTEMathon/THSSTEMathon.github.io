What is the THS STEM-a-thon?

The THS STEM-a-thon is a two day event hosted by several different STEM clubs of THS. Here, your kids will engage in eight unique STEM related activities, each designed for their enjoyment and education. The future lies in the fiels of STEM, and your children will get a taste of it.

WHO:

 Students in grades 4-8

WHAT:

 Participate and experience eight unique fun STEM Activities!

WHEN:

 March 13-14, 9:30 AM-2:30 PM

WERE:

WHY:

 Explore and engage in exciting fields of STEM!
